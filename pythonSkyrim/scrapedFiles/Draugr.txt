Dialogue[edit]
In addition to using the Thu'um, draugr often shout in the dragon language during combat. Some of these phrases include:

"Aav dilon!"
"Bolog aaz, mal lir!"
"Daanik Kendov!"
"Dir volaan!"
"Faaz! Paak! Dinok!"

"Kren sosaal!"
"Qiilaan us dilon!"
"Sovngarde saraan!"
"Unslaad Krosis!"
